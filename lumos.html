Lumos: A Smart Street Lighting System

Project information

  • Project date: 2023-Present
  • Project URL: Lumos

Lumos is an smart street lighting project that was designed to meet the particular demands of Sri Lanka, providing sustainable and energy-efficient lighting systems that's suitable to the Sri Lankan context. This project addresses the long-standing problem of insufficient street lighting, particularly in rural regions where safety and visibility have long been compromised . Lumos provides this through the use of modern technology and a user-friendly application that enables for remote management and monitoring. The automated notification system, which informs authorities to any faults, ensures timely repairs and minimal interruptions. Lumos is also environmentally aware, adjusting its brightness in response to changing weather conditions in order to maximize energy use. This project's goal is not just brightening the streets with Lumos, but also to set the path for a safer, more sustainable, and energy-efficient Sri Lanka.
